Took final Energy consumption of district heating in 2018 from "EU Commission: District Heating and Cooling in the European Union". Summed up Residential and Services to Buildings.
Took the development of the Buildings consumption from "Fallahnejad et al. 2024: District heating potential in the EU-27 (Best case Scenario: DH potential)". To do so, the Change between the 2020 and 2050 value was taken and applied to the consumption value of the EU Commission source.
Industry stays on same level.

See calculation file for the calculations.


Sources:
	- EU Commission: District Heating and Cooling in the European Union
		- Heat_District
	- Fallahnejad et al. 2024: District heating potential in the EU-27
		- Heat_District

	
In general:
	- Assumed 2020 = 2018. Interpolated values for the years not given in the sources.
